About Child Theme Guides

In order to allow our clients to have their own customizations based on our themes, we declare them compatible with child themes.Even further we encourage everyone to use a child theme to keep a better track of what is custom made and what is theme based.

Here is our Child Theme Example

This sounds like a promise

Yes, this is a promise from our side that we will keep our themes up to WordPress standards and we will always keep in sight that our products work well with the child theme version.

The Importance of a child theme

Even if the WordPress documentation about child themes covers up pretty much everything we want to explain let's identify the big points

  • Assets Management –– Always imagine that a user may want to remove or change assets like styles or scripts and try to not make them interdependent
  • Template Files –– We will try to break our templates as much as we can in order to simplify the template overwrite process.
  • Functions overloading –– Allow PHP functions to be overloaded by the child theme's functions.php file.
  • Internationalization –– The user can always have his own strings and he should be able to translate them
